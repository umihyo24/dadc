# Demon Army Dispatch Center

A browser-based monster staffing company management game. The player is a recruitment manager who chooses military contracts for the Demon King's Army, analyzes enemy intelligence, hires monster applicants, merges same-species employees when efficient, deploys a formation, and earns profit plus reputation through contract performance.

## Core Loop

1. Receive contract offers
2. Select a contract
3. Analyze enemy intelligence
4. Recruit monster applicants
5. Merge same-species units
6. Deploy formation
7. Auto battle
8. Review contract evaluation
9. Earn profit
10. Earn reputation
11. Invest in better staffing and contract options

## Win Condition

Reach company rank A, or complete the Hero Elimination Contract.
